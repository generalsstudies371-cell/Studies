CNT Act Notes - JPSC Prep

Section 3: Key Definitions

Term Definition (CNT Act 1908)
Mundari Khunt-Kattidari Means the interest of a Mundari Khunt-Kattidar.
Permanent Tenure A tenure which is heritable and not held for a limited time.
Proprietor Person owning an estate (in trust or for own benefit).
Rent Lawful payment (money/kind) by tenant to landlord.
Village Headman Includes Manki, Pradhan, Manjhi, Thikadar, or Ijardar.

üìò The Chotanagpur Tenancy Act, 1908

Covers Four Divisions:
1. Palamu (Medininagar) | 2. Kolhan (Chaibasa)
3. North Chotanagpur (Hazaribagh) | 4. South Chotanagpur (Ranchi)
Objective: Passed to protect the interests of aboriginals residing within the above regions.
Structure: Contains 19 Chapters, 271 Sections, and two Schedules.
Chapter 1 Sections 1 to 4
Identity, Area of Operation, Repeal of earlier laws & definitions.
Section 1 Name & Extent
Name: Chotanagpur Tenancy Act, 1908.
Extent: North Chotanagpur, South Chotanagpur & Palamu divisions.
Includes: Municipal areas, Notified area Committees, and Cantonment areas.
Section 2 Repeal
Repeal: Repeal earlier tenancy law.

Section 3 Definitions
Holding: A parcel or parcels of land held by a raiyat forming a separate tenancy.

Korkar: Land artificially levelled or embanked primarily for cultivation of rice.

Landlord: Person immediately under whom a tenant holds land and includes the Government.

Mundari Khunt-Kattidar Tenancy: Interest of a Mundari Khunt-Kattidar.

Permanent Tenure: A tenure which is heritable and not held for a limited time.

Proprietor: A person owning land, whether in trust or for own benefit, as estate or part of estate.

Rent: Whatever is lawfully payable in money or kind by a tenant to landlord for use or occupation of land, excluding personal service.

Tenant: A person who holds land under another and is liable to pay rent for that land.

Tenure: Interest of a tenure-holder including under-tenure, but excluding Mundari Khunt-Kattidari tenancy.

Village Headman: Headman of a village or group of villages, known as Manki, Manjhi, Pradhan or equivalent such as Tikkadar or Ijardaar.
